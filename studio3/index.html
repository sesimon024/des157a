Operation Treat Time!

You are on a mission with Cali and Charlie -the K9s of the UC Davis Police Department- to gather at least 30 biscuits without the handler catching you.

The player whose turn it is makes a journey of two trips to get biscuits. The total of the biscuits haul is added to the current K9's treat bowl, unless they only got one biscuit on either of their trips, then it means the handler was alerted before you could grab more. If this happens, the next K9 is sent in to try and get more treats.

After each set of trips, the current player can choose to make another two trips (assuming they didn't only get one biscuit on one of the trips) or if the K9 player feels that luck is running thin, they can send in their friend and take a well-deserved nap! The first K9 to get 30 biscuits gets to celebrate and eat all the biscuits.
