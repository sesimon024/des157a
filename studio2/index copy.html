Sofia's Photographic Memory

This is from when I went on a trip to Thailand with my parents, we were in a temple and there was a pond that had some bees pollinating. I remember feeling very happy and content

I remember that this was from when I went on a walk during valentines day. It reminds me now of how happy and loved I felt and how it was so interesting that I coincidentally saw heart-shaped flowers on that day.

I remember my parents and I going on a trip for my 20th birthday. We were at a revered park filled with black sand and this vibrant flower stood out to me. This reminds me of the great memories that I have with my parents.

I remember returning to Davis for the summer after returning from visiting home. I really enjoy walking through the arboretum, so I went through with my friend Shreya and we found these beautiful vibrant maroon and gold flowers. I remember feeling so happy to have such a good friend and enjoying our time together.

I remember flying to Michigan to visit my sister in Ann Arbor (where she goes to school). We went to her football game and I remeber having so much fun. I found this bee pollinating and thought it was so cool. I rememer having such a great time with her.
